CASH CROPS

🌾 Cotton Crop Information

🧬 Basic Information

Common Name: Cotton

Scientific Name: Gossypium spp.

Popular Varieties: MCU-5, Surabhi, Bt Cotton

🌍 Climate & Soil

Temperature: 21–30°C

Rainfall: 600–1200 mm

Soil Type: Loamy, well-drained

pH Level: 6.0–7.5

🌱 Sowing & Planting

Best Season: June–July

Seed Rate: 15–20 kg/ha

Spacing: 75 cm × 30 cm

Germination Time: 5–10 days

💧 Irrigation & Fertilizer

Watering: Every 7–10 days

Organic Fertilizers: Compost, neem cake

Chemical Fertilizers: NPK (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium)

🐛 Pest & Disease Management

Common Threats: Bollworm, aphids, whiteflies

Prevention: Crop rotation, resistant varieties

Treatment: Neem oil, Bt sprays, IPM techniques

🌾 Harvesting

Maturity Time: 150–180 days

Signs: Open bolls, dry leaves

Techniques: Hand picking, mechanical harvesters

📈 Yield & Economics

Yield: 400–800 kg/acre

Cost: ₹25,000–₹35,000/acre

Market Price: ₹60–₹80/kg

🌾 Sugarcane Crop Information

🧬 Basic Information

Common Name: Sugarcane

Scientific Name: Saccharum officinarum

Popular Varieties: Co 86032, CoC 671, Co 0238

🌍 Climate & Soil

Temperature: 20°C to 35°C

Rainfall: 1000–1500 mm

Soil Type: Loamy, alluvial

pH: 6.5 to 7.5

🌱 Sowing & Planting

Season: December to February

Seed Rate: 25,000–30,000 setts/acre

Spacing: 75 cm × 30 cm

Germination: 7–10 days

💧 Irrigation & Fertilizer

Watering: Every 7–10 days

Fertilizers: FYM, urea, DAP, potash

🐛 Pest & Disease Management

Threats: Shoot borers, red rot

Prevention: Seed treatment, rotation

Natural Remedies: Neem sprays

🌾 Harvesting

Maturity: 10–12 months

Signs: Yellow leaves, hard stalks

Techniques: Manual or mechanical

📈 Yield & Economics

Yield: 35–40 tons/acre

Cost: ₹50,000–₹70,000/acre

Market Price: ₹2,800–₹3,200/ton

🌿 Groundnut Crop Information

🧬 Basic Information

Common Name: Groundnut

Scientific Name: Arachis hypogaea

Popular Varieties: Kadiri Lepakshi, Kadiri-6, Dharani, TGCS-1694

🌍 Climate & Soil

Temperature: 25–30°C

Rainfall: 500–1000 mm

Soil Type: Sandy loam or loamy soil

pH: 5.5–7.0

🌱 Sowing & Planting

Season: Kharif (June–July), Rabi (Oct–Nov), Summer (Jan–Feb)

Seed Rate: 80–100 kg/ha

Spacing: 30 cm × 10 cm

Germination: 5–10 days

💧 Irrigation & Fertilizer

Schedule: Regular watering during flowering and pod development

Fertilizers: 15–20 kg N, 40 kg P₂O₅, 40 kg K₂O/ha

Organic: FYM, gypsum, Rhizobium inoculation

🐛 Pest & Disease Management

Pests: Aphids, thrips, pod borers

Diseases: Leaf spot, rust, stem rot

Natural Remedies: NSKE 5%, Trichoderma viride

Chemical: Chlorpyrifos, Mancozeb

🌾 Harvesting

Maturity: 90–120 days

Signs: Yellowing leaves, hardened pods

Techniques: Manual pulling or mechanical diggers

📈 Yield & Economics

Yield: 15–25 quintals/acre

Cost: ₹25,000–₹35,000/acre

Market Price: ₹5,000–₹7,000/quintal

🌿 Cashewnut Crop Information

Season: June to December
Spacing: 7m x 7m or 5m x 4m
Germination: 2–3 weeks for grafts
