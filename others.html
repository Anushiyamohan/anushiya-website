Idigenous dairy breeds of cattle

1.Gir

Category Details
Breed Origin Native to Gujarat; now raised in Tamil Nadu due to high milk yield
Milk Yield >Average: 10–15 liters/day; up to 25 liters/day in organized farms
Common Diseases Mastitis, Foot-and-mouth, Deg Nala disease (linked to poor fodder)
Treatment Antibiotics (Oxytetracycline), anti-inflammatory drugs, antiseptic creams
Fodder Green fodder (Napier, Sorghum), dry fodder (paddy straw), mineral mix
Lifespan 12–15 years; can produce 6–10 calves
Special Traits A2 milk, heat tolerance, disease resistance, gentle temperament

2.Sindhu

Category Details
Breed Origin Kangayam region, Tiruppur District, Tamil Nadu
Milk Yield 5–8 liters/day (some sources say 2.5–3.5 liters/day in native tracts)
Common Diseases Mastitis, Foot-and-mouth disease, parasitic infections, hormonal imbalance
Treatment Mineral mixture and salt licks (underused; only 31% provide them)
Fodder Homemade and commercial concentrates (used by 100% of surveyed farmers)
Milk Quality Rich in fat (4.5–5%), ideal for ghee, curd, and butter

3.Kangayam

Category Details
Breed Origin Composite breed from Sahiwal and other indigenous lines; developed for high milk yield
Milk Yield 12–15 liters/day (some cows yield up to 18 liters)
Common Diseases Generally disease-resistant; occasional mastitis or digestive issues
Treatment
  • Mastitis: Antibiotics, neem-turmeric paste
  • Green fodder: CO (CN)4 grass, maize, Napier bajra
  • Digestive issues: Baking soda, ginger powder, flax oil
Primary Use Dairy production; A2 milk yield
Milk Quality High butterfat content (~4.8–5.2%); ideal for ghee, curd, and paneer

4.Umbalachery

Category Details
Breed Origin Umbalachery village, Nagapattinam District; also found in Thiruvarur and Thanjavur
Milk Yield 6–10 liters/day (average ~494 kg per lactation)
Common Diseases Lumpy Skin Disease (LSD),Amphistomiasis:
Treatment
  • Fly bites: Neem oil + turmeric paste; fly repellents
  • LSD: Homeopathic remedies (Variolinum 200C, Thuja 200C), neem-turmeric paste
  • Amphistomiasis: Deworming, pasture management, snail control
  • Digestive issues: Baking soda, flax oil, ginger powder, magnesium sulfate
Primary Use Draught work in marshy paddy fields; moderate milk production
Milk Quality ~4.9% fat content; ideal for curd, ghee, and butter
